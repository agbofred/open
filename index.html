Welcome to my page!

I'm Fred Agbo

My professional career interest are mainly teaching and research, particularly, finding ways to enhance students' understanding of computer science using state of the art approaches. I'm originally from Nigeria and I obtained my PhD from the Unversity Eastern Finland. My teaching career started from Nigeria and then, I moved to Finland for research and teaching which led to my PhD, and afterward, moved to the US to join willamette community. I am proud to join the team in computing and data science at willamette University as an assitant professor, and I'm motivated by the diversity, students' activeness and demonstration of intelligence.

Intro to programming in Python is a course that focuses on introducing concepts of computer science and programming to students through the use of the programming language Python.
