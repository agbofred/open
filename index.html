Welcome to my page!

I'm Fred Agbo

My professional career interests are mainly teaching and research, particularly, finding ways to enhance students' understanding of computer science using state of the art approaches, for which I'm devoted to finding good practices in this regard. I'm from Nigeria and obtained my PhD in computer science from the Unversity Eastern Finland. My teaching career started in a Nigerian university and then, I moved to Finland to conduct research leading to my PhD. At the school of computing, University of Eastern Finland, I was responsible for co-designing learning environments with students to foster computational thinking and problem-solving skills. Besides, I was teaching and supervising graduate students' projects and thesis. Joining Willamette University as assistant professor in Computing and Data Science presents excellent opportunities for my career advancement and to continue to pursue my interest. Aside from professional life, I love God and am always enthusiastic about things of God, cherish and enjoy family life, and love to learn musical instruments.
